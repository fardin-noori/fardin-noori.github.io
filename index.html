About Me

I am Fardin Noori, born on March 31, 1992, in Kermanshah, Iran. I currently live in Tehran. Professionally, I have a strong interest in Power Systems, and I studied Power Electrical Engineering. My research areas include Power System Operation and Control, Power System Topology, Flexibility, and Reconfiguration. I am also interested in Renewable Energies and their impact on Power Systems. Language-wise, I am a native speaker of Persian, and I'm fluent in English. Let me tell you about my education. I completed my Master's degree in Power Electrical Engineering, specializing in Power Systems, at Shahid Beheshti University in Tehran, Iran, from September 2015 to September 2017. Before that, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering with a focus on Power Electrical Engineering at Shahid Rajaee University in Tehran, Iran, from September 2010 to September 2014. During high school, I excelled in Mathematics and Physics. I've had various roles at Tarh O Palayesh Engineering Company, including Project Manager, Project Section Leader, Renewables Engineer, Deputy Head of Electrical Discipline, and Electrical Engineer and Designer. I've worked on different projects, such as solar power plants, oil field development, and gas pipeline management. Apart from my job, I also taught courses related to solar energy and electrical engineering. I've recently worked on a research paper about Optimizing Grid Congestion Management with Cryogenic Energy Storage and Electric Vehicles, which is under review. My skill set includes industrial electrical system design, proficiency in software like MATLAB, Microsoft Office, and AutoCAD, and strong management skills. I've attended workshops and obtained certificates in various areas, including electrical engineering, safety, and language proficiency. Outside of work and studies, I enjoy literature, playing the Tanbur, exploring psychology and philosophy, watching movies and series, and listening to music. This is a brief overview of who I am and what I've been up to as Fardin Noori.
